## 3. Voice & tone

- **Confident, not boastful.** State outcomes ("redesigned a Shopify store for
  higher conversion"), never invented metrics.
- **Recruiter-first.** Lead with value and results. Skimmable.
- **Precise.** Prefer concrete nouns and verbs. No filler, no lorem ipsum, ever.
- **Global + local.** Written for UAE and international audiences alike.

## 4. Content rules

1. Every piece of copy improves on the source without inventing experience.
2. Any missing fact becomes a `TODO` in `docs/TODO.md` and a marked placeholder
   in code — never fabricated and never lorem ipsum.
3. Claims about clients/projects stay at the level the source supports
   (e.g. "50+ clients", "100+ projects" appear on the live site as headline stats).
